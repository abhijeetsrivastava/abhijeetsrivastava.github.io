Planning Phase

January 7, 2022 2-minute read
success

Planning Phase is the first step after one has decided to achieve something.

Why is a plan needed?

Ask this question to a contractor who is building a huge complex.

Although, in short you need a plan to know from where you starting and where you want to go and to layout a plan that would help you achieve it.

Steps

  1. Make a specific goal, which is measurable.
  2. Gather the resources which you would need.
  3. Make a plan on how to use those resources.
  4. (Optional) Have a mentor to guide you.
  5. (Optional though important) Have quick feedback sessions (like taking mock tests).
  6. Review your plan from time to time to see if your timeline needs to be updated.

Example

Let’s take the example of GRE exam.

  1. Goal: Clear GRE with minimum X marks.
  2. Resources: Books, Coaching?, Seniors who have taken the exam earlier.
  3. Plan: There could be 2 month plans/ 6 month plans/ 1 year plans. One can choose from many plans available online.
  4. Mentor: Seniors, teachers, mentors from coaching.
  5. Feedback Sessions: Mock tests.

Constraints

While making plans always be mindful of the constraints in your life. be it family/ monetory/ health related/ current job etc. Do not be too optimistic or too pessimistic while making plans. Make space for family/ current job/ health (mental health and exercise)

Outro

Make a plan that you can consistently stick to. Try and experiment this. Make plans often, fail often, try again until you find something that works for you. Do not ignore family, health as these are forever. Once you get into this habit you would find yourself more motivated since you can focus without worrying about any constraint.
